Should you use AI to write your resume?

Posted by Team SFS on September 19, 2024 · 1 min read

AI is changing the way we work, and it is also changing the way we write resumes. Should you use AI to write your resume? Let’s find out.

AI tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, and other are extremely useful to write your resume. They can help you write a resume that is tailored to the job you are applying for. They can also help you write a resume that is free from errors and typos.

However, you should be judicious in using AI to write your resume.

Your experience, work history and achievements are unique to you. AI tools can help you write a resume, but they cannot replace your unique voice and perspective.

Here are some tips to use AI to write your resume:

  1. Write down as much as you can about your work summary, experience, and achievements in your own voice.
  2. Use AI tools to refine your resume, correct errors, and make it more readable.
  3. Make sure generated output AI does not sound robotic. It should sound like you.
  4. When providing context to AI, ensure you highlight why a certain achievement is important and how it impacted the business.

AI tools can help you write a resume, but they cannot replace your unique voice and perspective.
