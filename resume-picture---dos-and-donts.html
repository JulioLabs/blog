Resume Picture - dos and don'ts

Posted by Team SFS on September 02, 2024 · 1 min read

A picture is worth a thousand words.

In most cases, a picture on a resume is not necessary. However, there are some places, for example LinkedIn profile, or some countries where it is common to include a picture on a resume.

If you decide to include a picture on your resume, here are some dos and don’ts to keep in mind:

  1. Make sure picture is recent.
  2. Make sure picture is clear and well-lit.
  3. Wear attire that is not necessarily professional, but appropriate for the job you are applying for.
  4. Make sure picture is not too casual or too formal.
  5. Make sure picture is not too close or too far.
  6. Do not include action shots, unless you are applying for a job where it is relevant for example a gym trainer.
  7. Do not include pictures with other people in it.
  8. Do not include a picture where you are frowning or making a silly face.
  9. Do not include a picture where you are wearing sunglasses or a hat.

Ideally, avoid including a picture on your resume unless it is required or common practice in your country or industry. If you do decide to include a picture, make sure it is professional and appropriate for the job you are applying for.
